Frequently Asked Questions

Is this game really free?

Yes! Click Game is 100% free with no ads and no in-app purchases. All features are available through normal gameplay.

Can I earn real money from this game?

No. All coins and currency in Click Game are purely virtual and have no real-world monetary value. This game is for entertainment only.

How do I save my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved every 30 seconds. You don't need to do anything manually!

Do I earn coins while the app is closed?

Yes! Your auto clickers continue working while you're away (up to 8 hours). You'll see your offline earnings when you return.

When should I prestige?

The longer you wait, the more prestige levels you gain. But prestiging earlier lets you benefit from bonuses sooner. It's a strategic choice!

How do I reset my game?

Go to Stats tab, scroll down, and tap "Reset All Progress". Warning: This deletes everything including prestige levels!

Can I sync my progress across devices?

Currently, game data is stored locally on your device only. We don't sync to iCloud or other services.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! We don't collect any personal data. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Tips & Tricks

  • Multipliers become very powerful at higher upgrade levels
  • Check back after a few hours to collect offline earnings
