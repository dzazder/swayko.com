  2. Set your goal ‚Äî Define your target weight in the settings
  3. Track regularly ‚Äî For best results, weigh yourself at the same time each day

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new weight entry?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen. Enter your weight and optionally add a note. The current date and time will be recorded automatically.

Can I edit or delete an entry?

Yes! Swipe left on any entry in the history list to reveal edit and delete options. You can also tap on an entry to view details and make changes.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or store any of your personal information on external servers. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

Yes, since all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your weight entries and settings.

Can I change the weight units?

Yes! Go to Settings and select your preferred unit (kg or lbs). All your existing entries will be automatically converted.

How can I export my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data. You can export your weight history as a CSV file that can be opened in Excel or other spreadsheet applications.

Tips for Accurate Tracking

  • Weigh yourself at the same time each day, preferably in the morning
  • Use the same scale for consistent measurements
  • Wear similar clothing (or none) when weighing
  • Don't be discouraged by daily fluctuations ‚Äî focus on the overall trend
  • Weekly averages are more reliable than daily readings
