Introduction

Brewing Calculator ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our homebrewing calculations application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All brewing calculations, settings, and preferences you input into the app are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any external servers.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your iPhone/iPad using the device's file system
  • Data remains on your device and is not synced to any cloud service
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored settings

Third-Party Services

Brewing Calculator does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Note that this app is intended for users of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@swayko.com.
