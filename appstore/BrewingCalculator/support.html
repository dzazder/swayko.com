Frequently Asked Questions

What gravity units are supported?

The app supports conversion between Specific Gravity (SG), Plato (¬∞P), and Brix (¬∞Bx). Perfect for both hydrometer and refractometer users.

How do I calculate ABV?

Enter your Original Gravity (OG) and Final Gravity (FG). The app calculates ABV using both Standard and Alternative formulas, and also shows attenuation percentage.

What sugar types are available for carbonation?

You can calculate priming amounts for Table Sugar (Sucrose), Corn Sugar (Dextrose), Dry Malt Extract, and Liquid Malt Extract. The calculator also accounts for residual CO‚ÇÇ based on temperature.

How does the yeast calculator work?

Enter your batch size and original gravity. The app calculates optimal pitching rates for dry yeast, liquid yeast (packs or starter), and slurry. It also considers yeast viability based on production date.

Which IBU formulas are available?

The app supports both Rager and Tinseth methods. You can add multiple hop additions, specify alpha acid percentages, and choose between pellets and whole hops.

What can I do with gravity correction?

You can calculate how much extract or sugar to add to raise gravity, how much water to add for dilution, or predict gravity changes after evaporation during the boil.

Is my data saved somewhere?

All your settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or store any of your data on external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Pro Tips
